About me

I study law in Norway and spend too much time thinking about ethics and philosophy. I’m particularly interested in emerging legal fields and international co-operation.

I have experience doing legal research for a nanosatellite project, and I was selected as one of two Norwegian representatives to the annual space law course hosted by the European Centre for Space Law under the European Space Agency.

I have also been invited as a speaker at the fourth international space law conference in Oslo, May 2026.
